How To Get To JSConf

Attending?

The very first batch of tickets will go on sale Monday December 16th. While there will be more tickets available than ever, they will sell out fast. Make sure you know the details and have made the necessary arrangements!

How do I attend JSConf US 2015?

Speaking?

Our call for speakers will remain open until Friday, January 10th, 2014 at 12:00PM EST. We strive every year to push the boundaries of what is possible with JS by spotlighting the future of the language and those making that future a reality.

Enlist now to help us change the world!

Sponsoring?

We do things a little different. There are no booths, no exhibit halls, and we encourage you to send your best technical talent. We focus on creating amazing shared experiences, which have far broader and longer lasting effects.

Enroll now to be part of the JSConf flight!
